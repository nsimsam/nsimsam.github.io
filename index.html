Hi, I'm Noor — a Syrian-Canadian medical student working at the intersection of equity and innovation. I'm drawn to how biomedical engineering, AI, and bioinformatics can improve care in underserved settings and for historically marginalized communities.

Having emigrated from Syria, I saw firsthand how conflict strains healthcare systems — an experience that shaped my interest in frugal, open-source biomedical technology. I co-founded the Canadian Muslim Women in Medicine Network (CMWIM) and spend my spare time tinkering with tech, biking, and losing at competitive Uno.
