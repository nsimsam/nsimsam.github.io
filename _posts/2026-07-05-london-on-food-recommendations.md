*Credibility: 15 years living in London, and a genuinely picky eater. Here's
where I actually eat.*

## Tacos & Shawarma

- **[Dos Tacos](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Dos%20Tacos%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best tacos in the city. Order the bang bang shrimp.
- **[Sultan's](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Sultan%27s%20Mediterranean%20Sarnia%20Road%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  on Sarnia &mdash; best shawarma. Ask for no pita, saj bread only, extra
  pomegranate sauce. *Skip Osmow's and Tahini's. Shelby's is fine in a pinch,
  but the quality has slipped the more they've franchised.*

## Hakka & Indian

- **[Hakka & Indian Cuisine](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Hakka%20%26%20Indian%20Cuisine%20Wharncliffe%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  ("Turmeric") on Wharncliffe &mdash; best affordable Hakka. Get the chicken
  tandoori momos, honey chilli cauliflower, and manchow soup. *Skip UberEats
  &mdash; it drops the quality.*
- **[Indian Paramount Restaurant](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Indian%20Paramount%20Restaurant%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best Hakka if you don't mind spending a bit more.

## Burgers & Fried Chicken

- **[BTRMLK](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=BTRMLK%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best chicken burger. Original or honey popper, both good.
- **[Chicken Plus](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Chicken%20Plus%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best Korean fried chicken. Puts the Wingstop hype to shame.
- **[The Works](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The%20Works%20Craft%20Burgers%20%26%20Beer%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  (downtown) &mdash; best beef burger. Free tomato, lettuce, and pickle
  toppings, fully customizable, bottomless fries. Guaranteed food coma.

## Wraps, Grills & Mandi

- **[Val's BBQ](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Val%27s%20BBQ%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  (downtown) &mdash; best tandoori chicken wrap. They make the bread fresh in
  front of you.
- **[The Shish](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The%20Shish%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best Arabic BBQ.
- **[Mandi Guys](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mandi%20Guys%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best Mandi.

## Brunch & Breakfast

- **[Makkan](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Makkan%20Restaurant%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best Arab food brunch spot.
- **[Eggstatic](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Eggstatic%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best general brunch spot. Fluffy pancakes and omelettes are the
  move &mdash; skip the Arab food here.
- **[Egg Cloud](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Egg%20Cloud%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best egg sandwiches. The creamy corn is a fan favourite.

## Bánh Mì & Sushi

- **[Thy's Banh Mi](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Thy%27s%20Banh%20Mi%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best bánh mì, plus a great $5 bubble tea.
- **[Heart Sushi](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Heart%20Sushi%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best all-you-can-eat sushi. Best value at weekday lunch, with an
  expansive lunch menu.

## Pasta & Pizza

- **[Mai's Cafe Bistro](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mai%27s%20Cafe%20Bistro%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best shrimp fettuccine alfredo.
- **[Through Thick & Thin Pizza](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Through%20Thick%20and%20Thin%20Pizza%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**,
  **[Zen'Za](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Zen%27Za%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**,
  and **[Pizza Madre](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Pizza%20Madre%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best pizza (three-way tie).
- **[Swan Pizza](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Swan%20Pizza%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best tandoori chicken pizza.

## Seafood & Small-Business Favourites

- **[Captain's Boil](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Captain%27s%20Boil%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best fresh seafood.
- **[WOW Pierogies](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=WOW%20Pierogies%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best (and pretty much only) pierogies. Small, family-owned,
  Ukrainian-run. Everything's made fresh so there's a bit of a wait &mdash;
  110% worth it.

## Group Dining

- **[Milestone's](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Milestones%20Grill%20%2B%20Bar%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  & **[Thaifoon](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Thaifoon%20Restaurant%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; affordable go-tos for when family visits from out of town.

## Bubble Tea & Dessert

- **[The Alley](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The%20Alley%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  / **[Gong Cha](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Gong%20Cha%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best bubble tea.
- **[Real Fruit Bubble Tea](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Real%20Fruit%20Bubble%20Tea%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best mango/kiwi bubble tea. They cut the mangoes fresh in front of
  you, no artificial syrup.
- **[Sugar Marmalade](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Sugar%20Marmalade%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  / **[Cherry's](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Cherry%27s%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best dessert spot. *Skip D Spot &mdash; expensive for a mediocre
  waffle.*
- **[Crown Pastries](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Crown%20Pastries%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best Arabic dessert.
- **[Mingle Mr & Mrs Bao](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Mingle%20Mr%20%26%20Mrs%20Bao%2C%20London%2C%20ON)**
  &mdash; best ice cream bao. Free board games, a great low-key hangout when
  you don't know where else to go after dinner.
